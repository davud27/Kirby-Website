Facts Of Kirby

Small Website By: David Garcia

This is Kirby, he is a little pink guy who goes around and beats up bad guys, saves the world, and other mischievous actions. You may know a few things about him, like how he is pink and or circular, but did you know that Kirby has power akin to a god (WOW!)? You heard that right, this pink guy can destroy the world at will, but he is too much of a chill guy to do stuff like that, so instead he chills, eats, and sleeps around most of the day.
